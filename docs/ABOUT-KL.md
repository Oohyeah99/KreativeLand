## Projects in This Ecosystem

### VocabVista
- Central API platform for vocabulary database, word list management, story generation, and media pipeline
- Serves as the backend for multiple frontend apps
- Built to power the entire ecosystem's vocabulary and content needs

### Little Learner
- Intended as KL's restart of the kids business in the early education space
- Born from teaching Kyler from infancy
- The new Little Reader and Little Classical Musician found at littlelearner.kids are the result of that experience
- This is the spiritual successor to BrillKids

### EnglishExamPrep (英考通)
- English exam practice app built for Kaley (KL's daughter)
- Targets Chinese students preparing for English exams

### ZhongkaoPrep (中考冲刺)
- Multi-subject zhongkao (high school entrance exam) practice app
- Targets Chinese students in Hangzhou preparing for the zhongkao exam

### LittleReader
- AI-powered bilingual storybook reader for younger ESL learners
- Consumes VocabVista's story generation and vocabulary APIs

### AIPlayground
- AI multi-provider interface (Express.js, vanilla JS)
- Supports Ollama, OpenAI, Gemini, ComfyUI workflows
- Serves as KL's experimentation platform for AI tools
